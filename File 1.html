The Story in the Book

Chapter 1

Molly had been starting out of her window for about an hour now. On her desk, lying between the copies of Nature. New Scientist, and all the other scientific journals her work had appeared in, was a well thumbed copy of On The Road. It had been Molly's favorite book science college, and the longer she spent in these four walls the more she felt she needed to be free.

She had spent the last ten years in this room, sitting nuder a poster with an Oscar Wilde quote prolaiming that "Work is the refuge of people who have nothing better to do", Although many considered her pioneering work, unravelling the secrets of the llma DNA, to be an outstanding achievement. Molly did think she had something better to do.
